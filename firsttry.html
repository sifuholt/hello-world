Senilda's Latin Cafe

appetizers Link 2 Link 3

After experimenting with a small soft drink and juice kiosk for a year, we decided in 1992 to create a cafe that served Creole food lunches prepared only with fresh products and the way grandmothers did in the traditional kitchen of Puerto Rico, in this way appeared the rice in the cauldron, the stewed beans with stir-fry and recao de la casa, the tostones, mofongos and stuffed avocados. Using this same principle, we developed the juice service and some mixes created by us and that reaffirm that our products are really natural and very tropical.
